# Welcome to Bellefeuille.xyz

![Lee and Kathy](/Images/Lee%20and%20kathy.jpg)

Bellefeuille.xyz is the central hub for our family—a place to connect, share, and grow together. Here, every member has a space to showcase their skills, share updates, offer services, and collaborate on projects. 

Our goal is simple: keep opportunities, resources, and knowledge circulating within our family, making life easier, more fun, and more rewarding.

---

## Explore the Family Hub

- **Family Tree** – Visualize our family connections and explore subpages for each member.  
  [View the Tree](https://dani.bellefeuille.xyz/tree)

- **Products & Services** – From baked goods to tutoring, see what our family offers and support one another.  

- **Funding Opportunities** – Help fund school trips, equipment, or personal projects, and track contributions.  

- **Events & Volunteering** – Stay updated on family gatherings, volunteer opportunities, and shared experiences.  

- **Members Directory** – Learn more about each family member, their skills, and ways to connect.  

---

## Why a Family Website?

Having a family website lets us:  
- Showcase skills and projects professionally.  
- Support family-run initiatives before seeking outside help.  
- Share updates, resources, and opportunities seamlessly.  
- Preserve our family history, memories, and achievements in one place.
